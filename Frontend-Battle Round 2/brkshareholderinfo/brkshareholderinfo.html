Common Stock Information

Berkshire Hathaway Inc. has two classes of common stock: Class A and Class B. Each share of Class B common stock has the same economic interests as a share of Class A common stock, except that a Class B share has 1/1,500th of the voting rights of a Class A share (rather than 1/10,000th prior to the 50-for-1 split of the Class B shares that occurred on January 21, 2010) and Class B shares are not eligible to participate in Berkshire’s shareholder-designated charitable contributions program (which was discontinued in 2003 for Class A shares).

For more detailed information about our common stock, including historical stock prices and dividend information, please refer to our annual reports or visit the SEC filings section of our website.
