Sustainability

At Berkshire Hathaway, sustainability is an integral part of our business strategy. We are committed to responsible investment and sustainable business practices, ensuring long-term value creation for our shareholders, employees, and communities.

Our key sustainability initiatives include:

  • Reducing greenhouse gas emissions and improving energy efficiency across our operations.
  • Investing in renewable energy projects and technologies.
  • Promoting sustainable practices within our subsidiaries and supply chain.
  • Engaging with stakeholders to address environmental, social, and governance (ESG) issues.

For more information on our sustainability efforts, please refer to our annual sustainability report or visit the relevant sections of our subsidiary companies' websites.
